DC Cloud Solutions Examination Portal


If you're trying to log into the DC Cloud Solutions Exam Portal, you're in the right place! We’ve simplified things with a single sign-on. Log into your DC Connect account here using the email ID and password provided to you by the company via email (the same credentials you received for your internship or exam access). You’ll still attend your assessments and submit your project evaluations through forms, but you’ll now access everything directly from your DC Connect account.

This portal will be used exclusively for giving exams and managing your internship evaluations.

  • If you’ve already logged in to DC Connect and/or created an account, use those same login credentials.
  • If you are new to DC Connect, follow the instructions for “Never Logged in Before” and use the email and password provided to you by DC Cloud Solutions (for example, the one that received your internship or exam notification). If you face any login issues, please connect with our support team for assistance.
  • If you are new to DC Cloud Solutions and DC Connect, follow the instructions for “New Candidate Register” to create your account and begin the process. You can also contact support if you need help completing your registration.
  • If you no longer have access to the email address you used previously, register as a new candidate and use the Request Merge option so our support team can help you recover access and merge your previous records.

Use your DC Cloud Solutions email ID and the default password which was provided in the email.
