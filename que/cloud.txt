Job description
GCP Cloud Engineer Internship

Company: DC Cloud Solutions

Location: Palghar, Maharashtra (Remote/Hybrid opportunities available)

Duration: 3 Months (Post Certification)

Start Date: As per certification completion

About Us

DC Cloud Solutions is an MSME-approved startup and a proud participant in the Google for Startups program. We specialize in cutting-edge cloud solutions, web development, and innovative training programs, offering aspiring cloud professionals the perfect platform to gain real-world experience in Google Cloud Platform (GCP) projects.

Role Overview

We are excited to offer a GCP Cloud Engineer Internship, a unique blend of training, certification, and practical work experience. This program is tailored for individuals passionate about cloud computing who want to accelerate their careers in Google Cloud Engineering.

Key Responsibilities

Training & Certification:
Participate in the Google Cloud Certification Training Path to build a solid foundation in GCP.
Complete the Google Cloud Associate Cloud Engineer Certification before starting the internship.
Hands-on Cloud Projects:
Design and implement cloud-based solutions for real-world projects.
Work on cloud infrastructure optimization, virtual machine deployments, data migration, and CI/CD pipelines using GCP.
Collaborate with experienced mentors to develop scalable and innovative cloud architectures.
Present a final cloud project at the end of the internship for evaluation.
What We OfferComprehensive Training:

Access to the Google Cloud Partner Learning Path with structured guidance.
Dedicated mentorship from industry experts.
Certification Benefits:

A Google Cloud Certification Exam Voucher (worth $200) to enhance your credentials.
If the exam is not cleared on the first attempt, free re-training and a second voucher will be provided.
Hands-on Internship:

A 3-month internship working on live cloud projects.
Real-world exposure to GCP technologies and opportunities to develop innovative cloud solutions.
Rewards & Recognition:

The Best Intern will receive:
A cash prize of INR 10,000.
A full-time job offer as a GCP Cloud Engineer at DC Cloud Solutions
Certification Support:

Continuous guidance and assistance to ensure successful certification completion.
Eligibility Criteria

Passionate about cloud computing with a strong desire to build a career in Google Cloud Engineering.
Basic understanding of cloud fundamentals with a willingness to learn.
Open to completing the Google Cloud Associate Cloud Engineer Certification before starting the internship.
Application Process

Enrollment Fee: Pay a nominal fee of INR 1000, covering administrative costs, participation certificate, and training resources.
Training & Certification:
Complete the Google Cloud Associate Cloud Engineer training.
Receive a certification voucher to take the exam.
If unsuccessful on the first attempt, free re-training and a second exam voucher will be provided.
Internship Begins: After successful certification, start a 3-month internship working on real-world cloud projects.
Project Submission & Evaluation:
Submit and present your final cloud solutions for evaluation.
High-performing interns will be considered for full-time roles at DC Cloud Solutions
