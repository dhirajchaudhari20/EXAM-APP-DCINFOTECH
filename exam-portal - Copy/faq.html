Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our exam portal and certification process.

You can register for an exam through the "Schedule Exam" link on your dashboard. Follow the on-screen instructions to select your exam, date, and testing center or online proctoring option.

If you are eligible for an exam voucher, it will be sent to your registered email address. Please check your inbox and spam folder. If you haven't received it, visit the Support Portal.

You will need a reliable internet connection, a webcam, a microphone, and a quiet, private space. You must also install the secure testing browser provided by the exam delivery partner. We recommend running the system check well before your exam time.

You can reschedule or cancel your exam through the exam provider's portal (e.g., Webassessor). Please be aware of the cancellation policy, as fees may apply if you reschedule too close to the exam date.

For most exams, you will receive a provisional pass/fail result immediately after completion. The official result and your digital certificate will be emailed to you within 7-10 business days.

For issues related to exam content or vouchers, please visit our Support Portal. For technical issues during an exam, contact the exam provider's support line immediately.
