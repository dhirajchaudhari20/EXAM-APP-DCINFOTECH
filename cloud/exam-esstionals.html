🎥 Watch Exam Do's and Don'ts

✅ Essential Requirements
  • Install secure browser
  • Strong internet connection
  • Camera and mic must stay on
  • No one else in room
📋 Testing Environment
  • Quiet, private, well-lit room
  • Remove all devices and papers
  • No food, drink, headphones
  • Do not leave camera view
🚫 Prohibited Actions
  • No whispering or talking
  • No screenshots
  • No breaks allowed
