Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2025

At DC Cloud Solutions, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how our chatbot handles your data.

1. What data we collect

Our chatbot may collect the following types of information:

The data is used solely for the purpose of improving our services and providing accurate responses. We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information.

We do not permanently store chat data. Temporary session logs may be used for debugging or improving responses but are automatically deleted after a short period.

4. Third-party services

Our chatbot may use third-party AI models or APIs (e.g., OpenAI, Google Cloud) to generate responses. These services may process your input but do not retain it permanently.

5. Your rights

You can request that we delete any identifiable information or stop processing your data at any time by contacting us.
